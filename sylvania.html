Off the beaten path tucked away from the hustle and bustle is a delicious homestyle Italian restaurant. You’ll find yourself picking off each other's plates and having one too many glasses of wine.

Beirut

Expect a homestyle experience here. Although the interior isn’t marvelous. Its philosophy seems to be that it should be more about the food, a philosophy which I like.

Mancys Steakhouse

Steakhouse $$$

If I could give you one good piece of advice when it comes to Toledo restaurants -- it would be to visit Mancy's Steakhouse.

Element 112

Bistro $$$$

French influenced, attractive plates, and cocktails to celebrate any occasion. The seasonal tasting menu experience is a must try for all you boujee foodies! So indulge yourself with an experience here.

Mancys Italian

Italian $$$

A continuation of the Mancy establishments, offering a friendly vibe with efficient service. It’s not a place I visit very often but it's an adequate place to wine and dine.

Plat8

American $$

A cozy atmosphere with a fine interplay between modern urban features and classically elegant touches. The fine and fresh ingredients serve your palate well.

Ameera

Mediterranean $

I think all Mediterranean restaurants are cursed with questionable decor but amazing food. You won’t have room for the homemade treats up at the counter but I highly suggest taking them to-go.
