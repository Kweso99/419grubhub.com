Mancys Bluewater

Seafood $$$

Taking you under the sea with an upscale atmosphere. Whether you’re indulging yourself in a multi-course meal or grabbing a seat at the bar it's ideal for dining with friends, family, and business associates.

Levi & Lilac's Whiskey Room

Bar $$

A lowkey place with high expectations. Isn’t much more you can ask for when sipping on a glass of whiskey in a charming historic building while being served a juicy burger. And don’t forget to grab your hangover cure in the morning at their coffee pub located across from the whiskey room.

The Village Idiot

Bar $

The beloved dive bar is a Toledo staple known for its funky decor and live music. If you go here without trying the pizza, you’re an idiot! Some say this place is an acquired taste, and I couldn’t agree more. So leave your high horse at the door and have yourself a beer sippin’, pizza fillin’, bass kickin’ good time.

Brew House Coffee & Bake Shop

Coffee $

While they offer a variety of baked goods and drinks the main reason I go here is for coffee and cheesecakes. Save your cheat day for the weekend because only then can you get their most delicious cinnamon rolls.

Barr's Public House

Bar & Grill $$

Serving up more than just pub-fare food with their hand-crafted cocktails, industrial-chic ambience, and prime location. This neighborhood spot is one of a kind. You’ll find the hipster millennials, business gurus, and run-of-the-mill housewives gathered here.
