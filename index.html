Whether you’re visiting or just looking for a new spot, here’s a cheat sheet into Toledo’s restaurant scene. Focusing on the locals and less on the chains. Which probably is just as well with some of the hidden jems we house here.

Current Addiction

Benchmark

Perrysburg

Toledo needed a restaurant like this with superb food, great atmosphere, and skillful staff. This place really is outstanding. Recommending something off their menu is nothing short of a challenge but I would have to say the chef’s way on a steak is badass.

Feeling Boujee

Element 112

Sylvania

French influenced, attractive plates, and cocktails to celebrate any occasion. The seasonal tasting menu experience is a must try for all you boujee foodies! So indulge yourself with an experience here.

Atmosphere

Rosarias On Third Street

Perrysburg

New to the restaurant scene and coming in hot with coastal italian fare, eccentric cocktails, modern vibes, and a gelato bar.

Cocktail Craving

Barrs Public House

Maumee

Serving up more than just pub-fare food with their hand-crafted cocktails, industrial-chic ambience, and prime location. This neighborhood spot is one of a kind. You’ll find the hipster millennials, business gurus, and run-of-the-mill housewives gathered here.
