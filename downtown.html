Bellwether

Cocktail Bar

$$

Extension of the Toledo Spirits Company. Now shows off how they can whip 'em up into craft cocktails in their quirky styled warehouse. The food situation here is limited, so get your bite in somewhere else.

Souk

Mediterranean $$

A solid spot to feed your instagram stories with sexy cocktails and eye-catching dishes. Personally fixated on the short rib dinner every time I visit. The charming ambiance is split between the two dining areas with exposed brick walls.

M Osteria

Italian $$

As most Italian restaurants it's easy to leave here overstuffed on pasta and wine. The chic warehouse style serves the restaurant well, setting the tone for a refined night out.

Kengo

Japanese $$$

Chef Kengo delivers the best high-quality authentic experience around. It’s first come, first serve so prepare to wait considering its small environment. Don’t forget to check out their Omakase as well.

Georgio's Cafe International

Fine Dining

$$$

For over 30 years they’ve been serving homemade style dishes. With a finessed flair of speakeasy meets mafia hangout, they know how to kick it old school.

Hannons Block

American $$

Even with its limited menu this place always hits the spot. They decided to kick it up a notch by opening the garage bar located in the back. Perfect addition to their already ideal atmosphere, location, food, and drinks.
